N°06Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need a fitness background to join? +

Not at all — every plan starts with a coach assessment so beginners and advanced athletes both train at the right intensity from day one.

Can I pause or cancel my membership? +

Yes. Every plan is month-to-month — pause, downgrade or cancel anytime from your member dashboard, no phone calls required.

Is nutrition coaching included? +

The Performance and Elite plans include custom nutrition planning. Starter members can add it on for ₹1,499/month.

What's inside the recovery lab? +

Cryotherapy chambers, infrared sauna, compression boots and an on-site physiotherapist for injury prevention and rehab.

Do you offer classes for teenagers? +

Yes — our Youth & Teen Athletics program is run by certified youth coaches with age-appropriate strength and conditioning plans for ages 13–17.

Can I try a class before joining? +

Absolutely. Your first session — whether it's a strength, yoga or HIIT class — is completely free, no card required.
