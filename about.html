N°01Our Mission

Fitness that respects
your time and body.

Stackly Fit was founded on a simple frustration: most gyms hand you a keycard and leave you guessing. We built a studio where every member gets a real program, a real coach, and real numbers to track — no guesswork, no gimmicks.

Today we run out of a 12,000 sq ft facility with a dedicated strength floor, group studio, recovery lab and nutrition kitchen — plus the Stackly Fit app that keeps your coach in your corner between sessions.

N°03Our Journey

12 years in the making.

2016

One Room, One Barbell

Stackly Fit opens as a 900 sq ft strength studio with a single coach and eleven founding members.

2019

Group Studio Launches

We add a dedicated cardio and mobility studio to meet demand for class-based training.

2022

Recovery Lab Opens

Cryotherapy, compression and an on-site physio team join the facility.

2024

The Stackly Fit App

Members get real-time workout, nutrition and recovery tracking synced to their coach.

2026

9,600+ Members Strong
