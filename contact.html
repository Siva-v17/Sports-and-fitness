N°02Common Questions

Before you
walk in.

Can I try a class before joining? +
Yes — every new member gets one free introductory session so you can meet a coach, see the floor, and try a workout before committing to a plan.
Do I need prior gym experience? +
Not at all. Our coaches build your first few weeks around your current fitness level, whether you're a total beginner or returning after a break.
What should I bring to my first session? +
Comfortable training clothes, a water bottle, and a pair of clean indoor shoes. We provide mats, equipment, and towels on site.
Can I pause or cancel my membership? +
Memberships can be paused for travel or injury, and cancelled with 30 days' notice. Reach out to our team through this contact form or by phone and we'll sort it quickly.
Do you offer 1-on-1 personal training? +
Yes, personal training slots run alongside group classes. Mention it in the message field above and our team will match you with a coach based on your goals.
